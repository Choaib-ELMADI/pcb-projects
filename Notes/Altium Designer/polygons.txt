=> Polygons or Copper Pours are solid areas of copper connected to a net.

=> Shortcuts:
    > Space             -> Change the direction of the polygon outline elbow
    > Left Click        -> Place a polygon vertices
    > Shift + Space     -> Change the corner style
    > Backspace         -> Undo vertices placements
    > Tab               -> Access the polygon properties
    > Right Click * 2   -> Complete the polygon creation

=> Settings:
    * Automatic repour settings:
        > Preferences > PCB Editor > General > ...
